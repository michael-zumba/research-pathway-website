PhD Preparation

Build the skills doctoral programmes look for.

Structured coaching and resources to help you develop the research capabilities that competitive PhD programmes expect.

1. The Research System

Understand how doctoral admissions and supervision work. Learn what committees and supervisors value.

2. Research Proposal

Learn to develop a compelling research proposal: framing a gap, designing a methodology, and building an academic argument.

3. Finding the Right Fit

Identify research groups that match your interests and learn to communicate effectively with potential supervisors.

4. Application Materials

Build a strong academic CV and supporting documents that reflect your research potential.

5. Interview Preparation

Practise presenting and defending your research ideas with academics who understand the process.

6. Transition Planning

Prepare for the academic environment: supervisory relationships, research culture, and setting up for success.
