Our Mission & Team

Built by academics, for future scholars.

The Founder

Dr. Yuqian Zhang

As an active academic in New Zealand, Dr. Zhang identified a key challenge: international students often find it hard to show their research abilities to New Zealand universities due to differences in academic culture, communication styles, and preparation expectations.

Research Pathway Limited was founded to close this gap—not by selling shortcuts, but by providing the mentorship and skill development necessary for genuine academic success.

Our Ethical Framework

We hold ourselves to the highest academic standards.

No False Promises

We do not guarantee admission or scholarships. Any service that does is likely misleading you. We guarantee high-quality guidance to give you the best possible chance.

Zero Plagiarism

We have a zero-tolerance policy for ghost-writing. We teach you how to write your own research proposal so you can defend it with confidence.

Conflict of Interest

Our mentors provide career advice, not pre-screening for their own labs. We maintain strict professional boundaries to ensure unbiased guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services, approach, and ethical framework.

General Questions

What is Research Pathway Limited?

Research Pathway Limited is a mentorship service founded by a New Zealand academic to help international students develop the skills and knowledge needed for successful PhD applications in New Zealand. We focus on capability building rather than admission guarantees.

Who is this service for?

Our services are designed for international students (including domestic students) who are considering or preparing for PhD studies in New Zealand. We serve students at various stages:

  • Early-stage researchers exploring PhD options
  • Students preparing research proposals
  • Applicants seeking strategic guidance
  • High-achieving students targeting competitive programs
How is Research Pathway different from other consulting services?

Unlike many consulting services that focus on admission outcomes, we prioritize:

  • Capability Development: We teach you how to conduct research, not just how to apply
  • Transparency: We clearly state what we can and cannot guarantee
  • Ethical Standards: Zero plagiarism policy and strict conflict of interest management
  • NZ Academic Context: Guidance specific to the New Zealand university system
Do you guarantee admission or scholarships?

No. We do not and cannot guarantee admission outcomes or scholarship success. Any service that makes such guarantees is likely misleading you. PhD admissions and scholarship decisions are made by university committees based on complex criteria beyond any consultant's control.

What we do guarantee is high-quality guidance, ethical mentorship, and skill development to give you the best possible chance of success.

Service Questions

What's included in the Foundation Course?

The Foundation Course includes:

  • Self-paced online modules with lifetime access
  • Comprehensive resource library (templates, examples, guides)
  • Email support for course-related questions
  • Focus on fundamental PhD preparation skills

It's designed for students who are early in their PhD exploration journey.

What does Guided Mentorship include?

Guided Mentorship provides:

  • 5 hours of one-on-one mentorship sessions
  • Comprehensive document reviews (CV, proposal, etc.)
  • Interview preparation and mock interviews
  • Strategic application planning
  • Ongoing email support

This service is ideal for students who are actively preparing applications.

What makes Premium Network different?

Premium Network offers:

  • All Guided Mentorship benefits
  • 60-minute faculty advisory session
  • Priority support (24-hour response)
  • Final application review
  • Strategic networking guidance

This tier is designed for high-achieving students seeking faculty-level insight.

Can I upgrade between service tiers?

Yes, you can upgrade from Foundation Course to Guided Mentorship or Premium Network. The cost of your Foundation Course will be credited toward the higher-tier service.

Ethical Questions

What is your plagiarism policy?

We have a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism and ghost-writing. Our mentors will:

  • Provide feedback and suggestions on your writing
  • Teach you how to structure and improve your work
  • Help you develop your own ideas and arguments
  • Never write content for you to submit as your own

You must be able to defend every word of your research proposal in interviews.

How do you manage conflicts of interest?

We maintain strict professional boundaries:

  • Our mentors provide general career advice, not pre-screening for specific labs
  • We do not have special relationships with admission committees
  • All guidance is based on publicly available information and best practices
  • We disclose any potential conflicts transparently
Are your services confidential?

Yes, we treat all client information with strict confidentiality:

  • Your documents and personal information are kept secure
  • We never share your materials or information with third parties
  • Mentorship discussions remain private between you and your mentor
  • We comply with New Zealand privacy laws and regulations
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services:

  • We offer a satisfaction guarantee for our mentorship services
  • If you're not happy with your experience, we'll work to make it right
  • We provide transparent refund policies for each service tier
  • Your feedback helps us continuously improve our services
