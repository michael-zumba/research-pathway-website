Why ProofPath

Editing should not slow you down.

Copying text between Word and a browser breaks your concentration. Generic grammar tools miss academic nuance. AI chatbots need constant context re-entry.

ProofPath stays inside your workflow. Select text, press Cmd+Shift+', and the AI proofreads it directly. Your changes appear side by side. Apply with one click.

System Requirements

macOS 12 (Monterey) or later

Microsoft Word for Mac

An API key from any supported AI provider

Features

Built for academic writing.

Word Integration

Talks directly to Microsoft Word. No copying, no pasting. Changes appear as a highlighted diff.

Multiple AI Providers

DeepSeek, OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, Groq, Perplexity. Bring your own API key.

Context-Aware

Choose your document type: PhD thesis, journal article, or general editing. The AI adjusts.

UK/AU/NZ Spelling

Built-in British, Australian, and NZ English preference. Reads naturally for Commonwealth standards.

Reviewer Comments

Generate reviewer-style notes explaining each change. Useful for co-authors and building your writing instinct.

Privacy First

Your text goes to your AI provider directly via your own key. ProofPath never stores or shares your content.

Installation

  1. Download the DMG for your Mac's processor.
  2. Open the DMG and drag ProofPath to Applications.
  3. Launch ProofPath. If macOS shows a security warning, go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and click Open Anyway.
  4. Grant Accessibility permission when prompted. This enables the global hotkey and Word integration.
  5. Open Settings and add your AI provider's API key.
